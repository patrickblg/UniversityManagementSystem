Architecture

This University Management System is a Spring Boot application built with Maven, designed to streamline academic administration. Its architecture features logically organized controllers to manage key entities efficiently.

Management

A unified Staff controller handles personnel, while Courses and Teaching Assignments are paired to reflect their logical binding. Independent controllers govern Departments, Rooms, and University data to maintain modularity.
